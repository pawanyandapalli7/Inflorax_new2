## HOW TO UPLOAD

1. Download or take photos
2. Rename them EXACTLY as listed above (lowercase, spaces allowed where shown)
3. Go to your GitHub repo
4. Navigate to public/photos/
5. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
6. Drag all photos in → Commit
7. Vercel redeploys automatically in ~30 seconds

If a photo is missing, the site shows a warm gradient instead — so nothing breaks.
